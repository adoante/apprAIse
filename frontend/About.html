Contextual image classification, a topic of pattern recognition in computer vision, is an approach of classification based on contextual information in images. "Contextual" means this approach is focusing on the relationship of the nearby pixels, which is also called neighborhood. The goal of this approach is to classify the images by using the contextual information. Wikipedia
